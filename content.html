About Amazon

Amazon.com, Inc. is an American multinational technology company focusing on e-commerce, cloud computing, online advertising, digital streaming, and artificial intelligence. It has been referred to as "one of the most influential economic and cultural forces in the world" and is one of the world's most valuable brands.

Here are some key aspects of Amazon:

  • Founded by Jeff Bezos in 1994.
  • Headquartered in Seattle, Washington.
  • Started as an online marketplace for books.
  • Now offers a wide range of products and services including electronics, apparel, furniture, food, toys, and more.
  • Operates Amazon Web Services (AWS), the world's leading cloud computing platform.
  • Has subsidiaries including Whole Foods Market and Zappos.
  • Produces consumer electronics such as Kindle e-readers, Fire tablets, Fire TV, and Echo devices.
